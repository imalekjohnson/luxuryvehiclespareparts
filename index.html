Genuine vs Aftermarket Lamborghini Spare Parts

Understanding part types is essential before making a purchase.

Genuine Lamborghini (OEM) Parts

  • Manufactured to Lamborghini factory specifications

  • Designed for perfect fitment and performance

  • Preserves engine reliability and resale value

Aftermarket Lamborghini Parts

  • Available for limited non-critical components

  • Lower upfront cost

  • Quality varies depending on manufacturer

For high-performance systems such as engines, gearboxes, suspension, and electronics, OEM parts are strongly recommended.
